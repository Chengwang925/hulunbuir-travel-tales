Life is an open wilderness.

When the noise of the city leaves you tired, return to the grasslands beneath a sky full of stars.

Hulunbuir Grassland Travel Tales is a narrative travel game about a seven-day journey across Hulunbuir. Explore grasslands, wetlands, forests, border towns, and local markets. Taste regional food, meet people along the way, manage your budget, and face unexpected events.

Every choice shapes your journey. There may be no perfect route—only the memories and stories you choose to bring home.

Features:

• A seven-day narrative journey

• Multiple routes, encounters, and endings

• Travel budgeting, meals, lodging, weather, and wellbeing

• Local food, landscapes, markets, and cultural experiences

• Chinese, English, and German language support



人生是旷野。

当城市的喧嚣令人疲惫，就回到银河下的草原。

《呼伦贝尔草原旅行物语》是一款以七日旅行为主题的叙事游戏。你将穿过草原、湿地、森林与边境城市，逛当地市场，品尝特色美食，认识旅途中遇见的人，同时安排预算、饮食和住宿，应对天气与意外事件。

每一个选择都会改变旅程。这里或许没有唯一完美的路线，只有你最终带回家的回忆与故事。

游戏特色：

• 七日旅行叙事体验

• 多条路线、随机奇遇与不同结局

• 预算、饮食、住宿、天气与旅行状态系统

• 当地美食、风景、市场与文化体验

• 支持中文、英文和德文
